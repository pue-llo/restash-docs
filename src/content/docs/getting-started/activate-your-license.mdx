import MailLink from '../../../components/MailLink.astro';

Restash uses **Lemon Squeezy** for license keys.

1. Open Restash and click the **menu-bar icon → ⚙️ Settings → License**.
2. Paste your license key (format: `xxxxxxxx-xxxxxxxx-xxxxxxxx-xxxxxxxx`).
   The field now fits a full key — no more cramped activation.
3. Click **Activate**.
4. You should see **"Activated"** with the email on the order.

**Where to find your key**

- Check the Lemon Squeezy confirmation email — subject contains
  `Restash` and your order number.
- Or visit `app.lemonsqueezy.com/my-orders` to download keys for any
  order.

**Lost your key?** Email <MailLink /> with the email address you bought
with — we'll resend.
