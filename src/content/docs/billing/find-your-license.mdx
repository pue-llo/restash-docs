---
title: Finding your license key
description: Where to look up a Restash license key from a Lemon Squeezy order.
---

import MailLink from '../../../components/MailLink.astro';

- Search Gmail / your inbox for **"Restash"** or **"Lemon Squeezy"**. The
  purchase confirmation contains the key.
- Or sign in at `app.lemonsqueezy.com/my-orders` with the email you
  bought with → click your Restash order → **License keys**.

If neither works, email <MailLink /> with the email address you used to
buy. We'll match the order and resend.
