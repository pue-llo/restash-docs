---
title: Moving Restash to a new Mac
description: Deactivate, reinstall, reactivate — and optionally copy your stashes.
---

1. On the **old** Mac, open Restash → **Settings → License** →
   **Deactivate this Mac**. Confirm.
2. On the **new** Mac, install Restash (see
   [Install](/getting-started/install/)).
3. Open Restash → **Settings → License** → paste your key → **Activate**.

**Want to keep your stashes?** Copy the contents of
`~/Library/Application Support/restash/` from the old Mac to the same path
on the new Mac (before first launch on the new Mac).
