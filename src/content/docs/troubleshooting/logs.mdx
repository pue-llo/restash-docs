---
title: Sending us your logs
description: Where Restash log files live and how to bundle them for support.
---

import MailLink from '../../../components/MailLink.astro';

If you've hit something we need to debug, attach logs to your support
email. They live here:

- `~/Library/Logs/Restash/main.log` — the app's main process log.
- `~/Library/Logs/Restash/renderer.log` — the UI window log.
- `~/Library/Application Support/restash/` — your data dir. Don't send
  the whole thing without checking it first — it contains your stash
  contents.

To collect them in one go:

```bash
cd ~/Desktop
mkdir restash-logs
cp ~/Library/Logs/Restash/*.log restash-logs/
zip -r restash-logs.zip restash-logs
```

Attach `restash-logs.zip` to your email to <MailLink />.
