---
title: '"License key not accepted"'
description: Common causes when Restash rejects a Lemon Squeezy license key.
---

import MailLink from '../../../components/MailLink.astro';

Most common causes, in order:

1. **Wrong email or key.** Copy directly from the Lemon Squeezy email —
   no extra spaces, no quotes, no truncation.
2. **Wrong product.** Make sure the key is for **Restash**, not another
   Lemon Squeezy product on the same account.
3. **Activation limit reached.** Each key has a limited number of
   simultaneous activations. Deactivate Restash on an old Mac (Settings
   → License → **Deactivate this Mac**) and try again.
4. **Network blocked.** Restash needs to reach `api.lemonsqueezy.com`
   during activation. If you're behind a strict firewall or VPN, allow
   that host or disconnect briefly to activate.

If none of those work, email <MailLink /> with:

- the email address you bought with,
- your order number,
- and the exact error text Restash showed.

We can re-issue or re-activate the key for you.
