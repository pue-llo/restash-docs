---
title: '"Restash is damaged" or "cannot be opened"'
description: Strip the Gatekeeper quarantine attribute, or right-click → Open to launch Restash.
---

Because Restash is ad-hoc signed (not yet notarised), Gatekeeper sometimes
quarantines the bundle and refuses to open it. Two fixes:

**Fix 1 — strip the quarantine attribute (one command)**

```bash
xattr -cr /Applications/Restash.app
open /Applications/Restash.app
```

**Fix 2 — right-click → Open**

1. In Finder, navigate to `/Applications/`.
2. **Right-click** `Restash.app` → **Open**.
3. Click **Open** in the dialog. macOS remembers this for the bundle.

Once notarised builds ship, this prompt will go away.
